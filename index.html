OUR STORY

How We Start Fiestapa

Fiestapa was born around a table just like the best stories are. We are four friends, each from a different corner of the world, who discovered a shared love for one thing: the magic of bringing people together through food.

From the lively streets of Spain to the cosy kitchens of Copenhagen, we found that Spanish tapas are simple, flavourful, and meant to be shared was more than just food. It was an experience.

The Heart of Fiestapa

Our journeys took us across different countries and cultures, but it was the warmth and vibrancy of Spanish cuisine that brought us closer. We realized that a single plate of tapas, no matter how small, has the power to spark conversations, create laughter, and forge connections.

With every bite, there's a story, a memory waiting to be made. That's why we created Fiestapa—to share the joy we've found in food and friendship.

Our Mission

At Fiestapa, our mission is simple: to create an authentic, unforgettable dining experience that connects people. Every dish we prepare, from the rich flavours of Iberic Ham to the simple delight of freshly baked bread, is inspired by the Spanish tradition of sharing food with loved ones.

Whether you're celebrating with family, hosting friends, or creating new connections, we believe that great food has the power to bring everyone around the table. We come from different parts of the world, but we share a singular passion—helping people build relationships, one bite at a time.

So when you choose Fiestapa, you're not just getting tapas; you're sharing in our journey, our stories, and most importantly, our love for bringing people together.

Order now and let us create a memorable tapas experience for you and your loved ones.
